Cream Biscuits

Cream biscuits are one of the easier recipes you could ask for. In a pinch, if you have heavy cream, you can make fresh biscuits for a large group of people in minutes.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • Melted butter
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
  3. Move to flat floured surface, knead a few times
  4. Shape into biscuit, or use ice cream scoop, brush top with melted butter
  5. Place on baking sheet
  6. Remove from oven when tops get golden brown (about 15 mins), brush with more butter
  7. Serve with jelly or gravy sauce
