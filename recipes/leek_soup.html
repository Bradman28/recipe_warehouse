Potato and Leek Soup

This might not be American.. I don't really consider myself a soup guy, but this will convert anyone who isn't a soup person. This recipe takes some time, but is very easy to make and super delicious. Can also be served cold, and in that case is called Vichyssoise.


Ingredients

  • 1 -1.5 lbs leek
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 - 2 medium potatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 6 cups chicken or beef stock
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 stick butter
  • salt
  1. Sautee leeks, onions, and potato in butter over medium/medium high heat. Salt lightly and add a little bit of olive oil too if you want
  2. Once all is softened, add stock and bring to a boil.
  3. Boil for about 12 minutes
  4. Let cool for a little bit, then blend FULLY in blender/food processor/ nutribullet. Make sure it is fully blended
  5. Put back in pot and add heavy cream. Stir and taste for any extra salt
  6. Serve plain or with crackers
